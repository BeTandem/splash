How Tandem Works

Select Times To Schedule

Creating availability schedules is finally simple

Easily select blocks of time that your network is able to host mentors. Want a recurring schedule? No problem.

Collect Mentor Availabilities

Reach out to mentors with a single button click

No need to individually email multiple mentors. With Tandem, facilitators can select multiple mentors and contact them all in one click.

Match Mentors to Mentees

Our intuitive scheduler makes mentor matching a breeze

Once the mentors have confirmed, the facilitator is notifed to assign times that work for both the mentor and mentee.

Automate Followups and Confirmations

Tandem makes sure your mentors and mentees can make their appointments

Cancellations and re-scheduling events are simple for Tandem. If a party cancels or re-schedules, Tandem will automatically adjust schedules and reach out to all parties to confirm the changes.
