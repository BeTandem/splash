What is a micro-network?

A micro-network is a group of mentors and mentees within an organization. Think everything from a small sports club to a tech accelerator, to a Universityâ€™s mentor program.

The micro-network disconnect

Mentors and mentees are not colliding enough within micro-network. This means that despite the strong potential for meaningful relationships between mentors and mentees, connections are not being made.

Building stronger micro-networks

Tandem allows network facilitators to quickly match, schedule, and get feedback about mentor/mentee meetings. As we enable more connections between mentors and mentees, stronger relationships are formed within the micro-network, leading to a stronger organization overall.
